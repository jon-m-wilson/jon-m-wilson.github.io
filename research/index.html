Throughout my studies I have been exploring cluster structures on non-orientable surfaces. Many questions posed to cluster algebras make sense for this modified setting, moreover, the answers often prove to be quite similar. For instance, here I show finite type quasi-cluster algebras have spherical exchange graphs.

Currently I am most interested in finding cluster structures which encompass both cluster algebras and the cluster structures arising from non-orientable surfaces.
