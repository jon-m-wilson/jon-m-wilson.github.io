Jon Wilson

I am a postdoc at Tübingen University under the Teach@Tübingen scheme -- lecturing a 2 semester graduate course on cluster algebras. My advisior is Christoph Bohle.

In March 2017 I completed my PhD at Durham University under the supervision of Anna Felikson.
