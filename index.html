Jon Wilson

I am a Lecturer in Mathematics at the Jeremiah Horrocks Institute , University of Lancashire.

Between October 2018 - March 2020 I was a postdoc at UNAM working with Prof. Christof Geiss and Prof. Daniel Labardini-Fragoso . Before that I was a postdoc at Tübingen University under the Teach@Tübingen scheme; I taught a two semester graduate course on cluster algebras (other such courses may be found on Fomin's Cluster Algebra Portal ).

In May 2017 I completed my PhD at Durham University under the supervision of Prof. Anna Felikson .

Broadly speaking, I am interested in the interplay between combinatorics, geometry, and representation theory. My research on cluster algebras and related structures can be found here.
