Jon Wilson

I am currently on the job market.

Between October 2018 - March 2020 I was a postdoc at UNAM working with Christof Geiss and Daniel Labardini-Fragoso. Before that I was a postdoc at Tübingen University under the Teach@Tübingen scheme — lecturing a two semester graduate course on cluster algebras.

In May 2017 I completed my PhD at Durham University under the supervision of Anna Felikson.

Broadly speaking, I am interested in the interplay between Combinatorics, Geometry, and Representation Theory. My research on cluster algebras and related structures can be found here.
